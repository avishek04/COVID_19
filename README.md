# COVID_19-WebApp-DotnetCore-MSSQL-JavaScript

This web application was developed during the COVID-19 pandemic to keep the community informed about the impact and changes happenning throughout the world because of COVID-19. Features of the application:
1. The Home Page shows tweets from top twitter(X) handle relevant to COVID-19, news from top publications all over the world and everyday live changes in the cases from your current country location and the world.
2. The Cases Analytics page shows graphical information of new cases and deaths data from all the countries and the world. It has a feature to compare two different countries data and how they have performed in controlling the damage. It also shows a list of data from all the countries.
3. The Vaccine Analytics page shows has similar format as that of Cases page but for vaccination data.
4. The News page shows latest news from New York Times and updates from top twitter handles related to COVID-19.
5. Survey page lets the use take a survey related to work from home. Once you select your option, you will be able to see the live results and opinions based on the survey.
